Case study · To Do App

Making daily task management feel lighter.

The app was designed for people who need a fast, low-friction way to capture and complete tasks without getting lost in features.

01

Problem

Many task management apps become cluttered as users add more tasks, making it difficult to prioritize work and stay organized.

02

Goal

Create a simple experience that lets users quickly add, edit, complete, and manage tasks with minimal effort.

03

Design solution

Used clear hierarchy, intuitive navigation, large touch targets, and instant completion feedback to reduce cognitive load.

04

My role

Competitive research, user flows, wireframes, interface design, interactive prototypes, and responsive HTML/CSS development.
